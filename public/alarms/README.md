# Alarm Tones

Add your alarm tone audio files here. The app expects the following files:

- `default.mp3` or `default.wav` - Default alarm tone
- `gentle.mp3` or `gentle.wav` - Gentle alarm tone
- `classic.mp3` or `classic.wav` - Classic alarm tone
- `digital.mp3` or `digital.wav` - Digital alarm tone

## File Requirements:
- Format: MP3 or WAV
- Duration: Should be suitable for alarm (30 seconds - 2 minutes recommended)
- Quality: 128kbps MP3 or 44.1kHz WAV recommended

The app will automatically loop the audio files when the alarm triggers.
